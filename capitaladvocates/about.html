About Capital Advocates

People, Values & Proven Partnership

Since 2003, Capital Advocates has partnered with organizations to achieve ambitious goals through strategic communication, change management, and government relations.

Our Story

Co-Founded in Saratoga Springs

Led by principals Bill Teator and Kim Parr Teator, we operate a lean, high-impact model that integrates the exact subject matter experts and trusted team members required for each client's unique solution.

Proven professionals in social media marketing and branding at Happy Brands Company and film professionals at 32 Mile Productions are trusted partners in bringing authentic identities and impactful stories to life.

We emphasize collaborative execution, backward planning from desired outcomes, and positive community impact — delivering results without the overhead of a traditional large agency.

Our Principals

Co-Founder

Bill Teator

With deep congressional and public affairs experience, Bill leads strategic communication and government relations initiatives at Capital Advocates. His background spans federal and state government, giving him firsthand insight into how policy is made and how to effectively advocate for clients navigating complex regulatory environments.

Bill served as the market brand development lead and introduction for the Saratoga250 nonprofit campaign, guiding the organization's branding, stakeholder engagement, and public launch to commemorate a pivotal moment in American history. His work there demonstrated his ability to unite diverse stakeholders around a shared vision and generate broad public support.

Bill's bipartisan congressional experience and robust professional network allow Capital Advocates to build bridges between clients and influential government sector decision-makers at both state and federal levels.

President & Co-Founder

Kim Parr Teator

Kim brings executive-level change management and strategic communications expertise to Capital Advocates. She has led industry-specific projects at Deloitte, designing and implementing transformation initiatives across complex organizations — delivering alignment, training, and measurable adoption at scale.

Kim's approach to change management is grounded in a deep understanding of how people and organizations process transformation. By combining rigorous communication planning with targeted training development, she consistently achieves 90%+ employee adoption rates in even the most complex organizational shifts.

As President, Kim oversees day-to-day operations and client relationships, ensuring every engagement reflects Capital Advocates' commitment to collaborative execution and measurable outcomes. Her leadership is defined by clarity, empathy, and a relentless focus on the client's success.
