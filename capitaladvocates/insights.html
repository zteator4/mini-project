Backward Planning: The Key to Effective Change Management

Most change initiatives fail not because the destination is wrong, but because the path is poorly mapped. At Capital Advocates, backward planning — starting with the desired end state and working step-by-step to the present — is the foundation of every transformation we lead.

Drawing on Kim Parr Teator's experience leading industry-specific change initiatives at Deloitte, this approach consistently achieves adoption rates that exceed industry benchmarks by ensuring every stakeholder receives the right message, at the right time, through the right channel.

Kim Parr Teator President & Co-Founder · January 2026
