Saratoga250 — Building Community Pride for America's Turning Point

Key Result Successful Public Launch
  • Service: Strategic Communication
  • Service: Brand Development
  • Service: Social Media & Video

The Challenge

Position a major historical commemoration — the Saratoga250 — for broad public engagement across diverse community stakeholder groups. The nonprofit needed a brand identity and launch strategy that honored the significance of the Battle of Saratoga while resonating with modern audiences across multiple platforms.

  • ✓ Strong media coverage across regional outlets
  • ✓ Sustained community support and stakeholder alignment
