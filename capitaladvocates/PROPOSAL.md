# PROPOSAL.md — Capital Advocates, LLC Website Redesign

## Client Brief

**Client:** Bill & Kim Teator, Capital Advocates LLC — a strategic communication, change management, and government relations consulting firm founded in 2003 and based in Saratoga Springs, NY.

**Purpose:** To provide a modern, authoritative digital presence that showcases the firm's expertise in government relations, strategic consulting, and advocacy for high-stakes clients — replacing an outdated GoDaddy site with a professional, conversion-oriented experience.

**Audience:** Corporate executives, non-profit leaders, and government officials who need to verify the firm's credibility and understand specific service offerings before initiating a consultation.

**Key action:** Review Capital Advocates' core capabilities and initiate a consultation via the contact section.

---

## Pages/Sections

- **Home** (`index.html`): Hero with value proposition, "Our Approach" process steps, core services teasers, social media & video content section, featured impact (case study cards), meet the principals, CTA.
- **About Us** (`about.html`): Firm story, principal bios (Bill Teator, Kim Parr Teator), our values, trusted partner network.
- **Services** (`services.html`): Full detail on all 4 core services + dedicated social media & video section, delivery model process.
- **Our Impact** (`case-studies.html`): 4 case studies with Challenge / Approach / Results structure.
- **Insights** (`insights.html`): 6 thought leadership articles, resource library, newsletter signup.
- **Contact** (`contact.html`): Contact form with service selection, principal mini-cards, embedded Google Map.

## Style Preferences

- **Colors:** Whites, greys, light blues — drawn directly from the Capital Advocates logo palette (Navy `#0d2d52`, Blue `#1a6bb5`, Sky `#4eb3e8`)
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (authoritative serif for headings), Lato (clean, readable sans-serif for body)
- **Tone:** Sophisticated, modern, professional — no "agency gimmicks," let the work and credentials speak
